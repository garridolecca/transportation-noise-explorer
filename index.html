This map visualizes transportation noise levels across the United States from rail, road, and aviation sources (2020 data). Noise is measured in decibels (dB) at ~30m pixel resolution. Click anywhere on the noise layer to inspect the local noise value.

Classified groups noise into EPA/WHO health-impact categories. Continuous shows the smooth gradient of noise values. Shaded Relief treats noise as terrain - louder areas rise as peaks.
